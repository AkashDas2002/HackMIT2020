Other Exercises

Fun Facts

  1. A general half-hour strength training session burns an average of 90 calories for a 125-pound person!
  2. Laughing for 10 minutes can make you burn between 20 and 40 Calories!
  3. Hugging for one hour can burn up to 70 calories!
  4. Pushing a shopping trolley up the aisles for half an hour will burn over 100 Calories, this number increases with the amount you put in your trolley and the heavier it gets!
  5. Singing in the shower can burn 10-20 calories per song depending on how loud you sing and your level of pitch!
