Fun Facts

  1. When you swim, you still sweat in the pool.
  2. Swimming uses nearly every muscle in your body - more than baseball and football. It’s a pretty awesome sport!
  3. Over half of Americans can’t swim so you’re already ahead of all of them if you can!
  4. The first goggles were crafted out of tortoise shells.
  5. If you haven’t tried them out, swimming flippers are always a super fun way to speed through the water and they were invented by none other than Benjamin Franklin himself!
